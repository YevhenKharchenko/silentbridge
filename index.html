Hashi:
Bridge Puzzles

Become the Island King
Get really good at connecting those bridges
So this is Hashi - basically you're connecting islands with bridges, and honestly? It's way more addictive than it sounds.

You've gotta connect these islands based on the numbers, making sure everyone can get around your little kingdom.

Simple rules.
Deep logic.
Endless mastery.

How to Play

Simple Rules, Endless Logic

Connect numbered islands with bridges matching the number on each island
Maximum 2 bridges between any two islands
Bridges can’t cross islands or other bridges
All islands must be connected in one continuous kingdom
It starts easy.
Then logic takes over.

Build Your Kingdom

Make sure every island is connected

Every puzzle throws something different at you, so you've gotta really think through each move.

You’re not just placing bridges —
you’re designing a functional kingdom.

One mistake can isolate an island.
One smart deduction can unlock the entire board.
The puzzles are challenging and will encourage you to think outside the box.
